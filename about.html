ERT Lighthouse Limited was set up in 2016 as an infrastructure, Energy and Mining Company, committed to Economic Development and Urban Development.

Tytron Exploration Limited is a subsidiary of ERT Lighthouse Limited set up to exclusively explore and develop the mining opportunities that abound in Sub-Saharan Africa.

To be the future.

Developing Communities by re-defining mining.

Our Purpose

We will contribute to the wellbeing of the communities where we operate and in line with our triple P strategy – People, Planet and Profit, we will better connect the resources in the ground to the people who need and value them. Our metals are the key elements in smartphones, electric cars and wind turbines, while also evolving traditional automobile and other machines, to deliver a cleaner energy. Our products build our homes, offices, railways and airports and our gold and diamonds fulfil your dreams. We are the future.

Our Strategy

Explore

Ensure commercial optimization by identifying and exploring fields with strong potential.

Develop

Optimally advance our licenses to project commissioning and delivery of optimal cash floes and returns.

Operate

Efficiently operate our assets for maximum return.

Grow

Continue to seek ways to achieve our triple P (People, Planet, Profit) growth.

Building Our Future

Our value creation strategy is linked to the future opportunities. We believe that the demand for minerals will grow in the coming years and we are aware of the trends that will shape the future.
Our GAME plan will be shaped by:

  • Best possible mine design and productivity scheduling
  • Capital investment
  • Operational cost
  • Revenue forecasting and
  • Management of cash flow for a mine operation
